FLATS

Flats are a type of residential property that consist of one or more rooms on a single floor of a building. Flats can vary in size, design, layout, and amenities, depending on the location, budget, and preference of the owner or tenant. Flats can be rented or owned, and can be part of a larger complex or a standalone structure. Flats are also known as apartments, units, or condos in different parts of the world.
